About us

We are Krishna Interiors.

A well-established interiors firm where every craft lives under one roof.

Krishna Interiors offers home and office furniture along with complete interior solutions for government organisations, laboratories, pharma & healthcare establishments, education & training institutes, auditoriums, sports clubs and stadiums.

Our services span the taking up and execution of furniture works, false ceilings, partitions, aluminium works, Venetian & vertical blinds, paintings and a wide range of other interior works.

We are experts in all types of shelters and customised UPVC windows and modular kitchen cupboards — made in our own manufacturing units located in Vijayawada and Bangalore.
